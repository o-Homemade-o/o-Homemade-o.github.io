MILKSHAKE

0 Hours 5 minutes
Ingredients:

  • 4 Scoops Of Vanilla Ice Cream(or any type of Ice Cream You want)


  • 1/4 Cup of Milk


  • Whipped Cream for decoration


  • Sprinkles! For decoration


  • Maraschino cherry for decoration


  • Add any sweet you want




Materials:
  • Scoop


  • Blender


  • Fancy Glass cup


  • Cute Straw (Optioal)


Procedure
  1. Put 4 scoops of Ice cream into the blender


  2. Add the milk into the blender with the Ice Cream


  3. Blend the ixure for about 3 minutes until your ice cream is melted and mixed with the milk


  4. Add you decorations


ENJOY!!
